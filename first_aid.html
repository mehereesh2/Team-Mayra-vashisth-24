First Aid Instructions

CPR

Perform CPR if the person is not breathing and has no pulse. Call 911 and start chest compressions and rescue breaths.

Burn

For burns, cool the burn with cold running water for at least 10 minutes and cover with a sterile dressing.

Bleeding

Apply direct pressure on the wound with a clean cloth or bandage. Elevate the injured area if possible.

Snake Bite

Keep the person still and immobilize the bitten area. Do not cut or suck the wound. Seek immediate medical attention.
